# Skybox Sources

These 1024x512 JPG panoramas are resized tonemapped HDRI previews from Poly Haven.
Each panorama was converted into six 512x512 cube faces (`front`, `back`, `left`, `right`, `top`, `bottom`) for the Minecraft-style preview skyboxes.

Poly Haven publishes assets under CC0:
https://polyhaven.com/license

- `venice_sunset.jpg`: https://polyhaven.com/a/venice_sunset
- `snowy_field.jpg`: https://polyhaven.com/a/snowy_field
